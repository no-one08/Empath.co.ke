Application Requirements

The following documents are required for your Luxembourg work permit. We prepare them all for you.

1
Europass Curriculum Vitae (CV)
An EU-standard Europass CV in English or French detailing your work history, education, and skills.
Required
2
Cover Letter
A professional letter of motivation addressed to the Luxembourg employer outlining your suitability.
Required
3
Police Clearance Certificate
A clean criminal record certificate issued by your national police or relevant authority, not older than 3 months.
Required
4
Medical Certificate
A fitness-for-work medical certificate from a certified doctor confirming you are in good health.
Required
5
Valid Passport
A valid international passport with at least 12 months remaining before expiry.
